How can I find a roommate?


Glad you asked!

Welcome to Find Your Roommate! Made by and for Duke students. Whether you are a freshman or senior, we are home to a database of students looking for roommates. Why bother yourself by scouring through the endless Duke Facebook posts or hoping to get a response from a DukeList ad? Here at Find Your Roommate, we have all the resources you need in a centralized location.

Register an account with us, fill out your preferences, and you will be able to find your closest roommate matches.
